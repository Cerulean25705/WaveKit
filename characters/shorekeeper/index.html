Simple play pattern

  • Build enough Energy Regen so Shorekeeper can heal before panic moments.
  • Use support/healing setup first, then leave field time for the damage dealer.
  • Prioritise consistency over perfect damage if you are still learning fights.

Shorekeeper FAQ

What is the best Shorekeeper build in Wuthering Waves?

Shorekeeper's recommended WaveKit setup is Healer-Support Build, using Stellar Symphony, Rejuvenating Glow, and Fallacy of No Return as the main Echo target.

What stats should Shorekeeper use?

Start with Healing Bonus or CRIT DMG / Energy Regen or HP% / HP%. If the rotation feels uncomfortable, improve Energy Regen or defensive comfort before chasing perfect substats.

What teams work with Shorekeeper?

Shorekeeper is flexible, so the best team depends on the Resonators and weapons you own.

Is Shorekeeper beginner friendly?

Shorekeeper's current difficulty is listed as Low stress. If you are learning, pair them with safer sustain or defensive support.
