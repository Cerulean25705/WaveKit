Sigrika FAQ

What is the best Sigrika build in Wuthering Waves?

Sigrika's recommended WaveKit setup is Echo Skill Aero DPS Build, using Solsworn Ciphers, Sound of True Name, and Nameless Explorer as the main Echo target.

What stats should Sigrika use?

Start with Crit DMG or CRIT Rate / Energy Regen or ATK% / ATK%. If the rotation feels uncomfortable, improve Energy Regen or defensive comfort before chasing perfect substats.

What teams work with Sigrika?

A strong reference shell is Sigrika / Lucilla / Shorekeeper. WaveKit can also suggest owned alternatives through the team helper.

Is Sigrika beginner friendly?

Sigrika's current difficulty is listed as Moderate. If you are learning, pair them with safer sustain or defensive support.
