Hiyuki FAQ

What is the best Hiyuki build in Wuthering Waves?

Hiyuki's recommended WaveKit setup is Glacio Chafe DPS Build, using Frostburn, Wishes of Quiet Snowfall, and Reminiscence: Threnodian - Voidborne Construct as the main Echo target.

What stats should Hiyuki use?

Start with Crit Rate or Crit DMG / Glacio DMG or ATK% / ATK%. If the rotation feels uncomfortable, improve Energy Regen or defensive comfort before chasing perfect substats.

What teams work with Hiyuki?

A strong reference shell is Hiyuki / Lucilla / Chisa. WaveKit can also suggest owned alternatives through the team helper.

Is Hiyuki beginner friendly?

Hiyuki's current difficulty is listed as Moderate. If you are learning, pair them with safer sustain or defensive support.
