Roccia FAQ

What is the best Roccia build in Wuthering Waves?

Roccia's recommended WaveKit setup is Havoc Sub-DPS Build, using Tragicomedy, Midnight Veil, and Nightmare: Impermanence Heron as the main Echo target.

What stats should Roccia use?

Start with CRIT Rate or CRIT DMG / Havoc DMG or Energy Regen / ATK%. If the rotation feels uncomfortable, improve Energy Regen or defensive comfort before chasing perfect substats.

What teams work with Roccia?

Roccia is flexible, so the best team depends on the Resonators and weapons you own.

Is Roccia beginner friendly?

Roccia's current difficulty is listed as Flexible. If you are learning, pair them with safer sustain or defensive support.
