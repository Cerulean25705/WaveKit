  • If the team feels stressful, use the helper to choose a real healer or safer third slot.

Zani FAQ

What is the best Zani build in Wuthering Waves?

Zani's recommended WaveKit setup is Spectro Frazzle Main DPS Build, using Blazing Justice, Eternal Radiance, and Capitaneus as the main Echo target.

What stats should Zani use?

Start with CRIT Rate or CRIT DMG / Spectro DMG / ATK%. If the rotation feels uncomfortable, improve Energy Regen or defensive comfort before chasing perfect substats.

What teams work with Zani?

A recommended team is Zani / Phoebe / Shorekeeper. WaveKit can also suggest owned alternatives through the team helper.

Is Zani beginner friendly?

Zani's current difficulty is listed as Moderate. If you are learning, pair them with safer sustain or defensive support.
