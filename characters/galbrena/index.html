Galbrena FAQ

What is the best Galbrena build in Wuthering Waves?

Galbrena's recommended WaveKit setup is Fusion Echo Skill DPS Build, using Lux & Umbra, Flamewing's Shadow, Flaming Clawprint, and Corrosaurus as the main Echo target.

What stats should Galbrena use?

Start with Crit Rate or Crit DMG / Fusion DMG / ATK%. If the rotation feels uncomfortable, improve Energy Regen or defensive comfort before chasing perfect substats.

What teams work with Galbrena?

A strong reference shell is Galbrena / Lucilla / Shorekeeper. WaveKit can also suggest owned alternatives through the team helper.

Is Galbrena beginner friendly?

Galbrena's current difficulty is listed as Moderate. If you are learning, pair them with safer sustain or defensive support.
