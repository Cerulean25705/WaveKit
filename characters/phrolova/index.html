Phrolova guide overview

Phrolova is a Havoc Main DPS in Wuthering Waves. Its recommended build starts with Lethean Elegy, while its Echo plan uses Dream of the Lost, Havoc Eclipse. The guide also covers stat priorities, compatible teammates, and a practical rotation.

Phrolova FAQ

What is the best Phrolova build in Wuthering Waves?

Phrolova's recommended WaveKit setup is Havoc DPS Build, using Lethean Elegy, Dream of the Lost, Havoc Eclipse, and Nightmare: Hecate as the main Echo target.

What stats should Phrolova use?

Start with Crit DMG or CRIT Rate / Havoc DMG / ATK%. If the rotation feels uncomfortable, improve Energy Regen or defensive comfort before chasing perfect substats.

What teams work with Phrolova?

A recommended team is Phrolova / Cantarella / Qiuyuan. WaveKit can also suggest owned alternatives through the team helper.

Is Phrolova beginner friendly?

Phrolova's current difficulty is listed as Moderate. If you are learning, pair them with safer sustain or defensive support.
