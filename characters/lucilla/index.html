Lucilla FAQ

What is the best Lucilla build in Wuthering Waves?

Lucilla's recommended Glacio Chafe setup uses Freeze Frame, Wishes of Quiet Snowfall, and Glommoth. Echo Skill teams use a different support set.

What stats should Lucilla use?

Start with CRIT Rate or CRIT DMG / Glacio DMG or ATK% / ATK%. Lucilla normally needs no extra Energy Regen.

What teams work with Lucilla?

Lucilla supports Hiyuki in Glacio Chafe and can switch builds for Echo Skill carries such as Sigrika, Galbrena, and Phrolova.

Does Lucilla always use the same Echo set?

No. Use Wishes of Quiet Snowfall with Hiyuki, Moonlit Clouds with Sigrika or Galbrena, and the Dream of the Lost route with Phrolova.
