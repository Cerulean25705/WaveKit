Rover guide overview

Rover is a multi-form Hybrid DPS in Wuthering Waves. The recommended build changes with the active element, so this guide compares weapon, Echo, stat, teammate, and rotation choices for Spectro, Havoc, Aero, and Electro Rover.

Rover forms

Spectro Rover
Flexible account anchor and Spectro/Frazzle-friendly option.
Havoc Rover
Damage-focused form for players who want Rover as a familiar carry or quick-swap unit.
Aero Rover
Accessible Aero utility for Erosion teams, but not a true healer.
Electro Rover
Version 3.5 sub-DPS/support form with team ATK utility. Current teams are still developing, so WaveKit keeps its recommendations conservative.

In the team helper, select the Rover forms you own and choose the active form you want WaveKit to use for suggestions.

Rover FAQ

What is the best Rover build in Wuthering Waves?

Rover's recommended WaveKit setup is Flexible Rover Build, using Emerald of Genesis, Moonlit Clouds or matching element set, and Impermanence Heron or Dreamless as the main Echo target.

What stats should Rover use?

Start with CRIT Rate or CRIT DMG / Element DMG or Energy Regen / ATK%. If the rotation feels uncomfortable, improve Energy Regen or defensive comfort before chasing perfect substats.

What teams work with Rover?

Rover is flexible, so the best team depends on the Resonators and weapons you own.

Is Rover beginner friendly?

Rover's current difficulty is listed as Beginner friendly. If you are learning, pair them with safer sustain or defensive support.
