      Simple play pattern

        Build priority

        Rover is most valuable when their buff, element, or mechanic matches the carry you want to build.

        Rover FAQ

        What is the best Rover build in Wuthering Waves?

        Rover's recommended WaveKit setup is Flexible Rover Build, using Emerald of Genesis, Moonlit Clouds or matching element set, and Impermanence Heron or Dreamless as the main Echo target.

        What stats should Rover use?

        Start with CRIT Rate or CRIT DMG / Element DMG or Energy Regen / ATK%. If the rotation feels uncomfortable, improve Energy Regen or defensive comfort before chasing perfect substats.

        What teams work with Rover?

        Rover is flexible, so the best team depends on the Resonators and weapons you own.

        Is Rover beginner friendly?

        Rover's current difficulty is listed as Beginner friendly. If you are learning, pair them with safer sustain or defensive support.
