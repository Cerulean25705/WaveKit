Lucy FAQ

What is the best Lucy build in Wuthering Waves?

Lucy's recommended WaveKit setup is Heavy Attack DMG DPS Build, using Spectral Trigger, Shadow of Shattered Dreams, Rite of Gilded Revelation, Celestial Light, and Reminiscence: Nightmare Adam Smasher as the main Echo target.

What stats should Lucy use?

Start with Crit Rate or Crit DMG / Spectro DMG / ATK%. If the rotation feels uncomfortable, improve Energy Regen or defensive comfort before chasing perfect substats.

What teams work with Lucy?

A strong reference shell is Lucy / Rebecca / Mornye. WaveKit can also suggest owned alternatives through the team helper.

Is Lucy beginner friendly?

Lucy's current difficulty is listed as Beginner friendly. If you are learning, pair them with safer sustain or defensive support.
