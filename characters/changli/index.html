Changli FAQ

What is the best Changli build in Wuthering Waves?

Changli's recommended WaveKit setup is Fusion DMG Build, using Blazing Brilliance, Molten Rift, and Nightmare: Inferno Rider as the main Echo target.

What stats should Changli use?

Start with CRIT Rate or CRIT DMG / Fusion DMG / ATK%. If the rotation feels uncomfortable, improve Energy Regen or defensive comfort before chasing perfect substats.

What teams work with Changli?

A recommended team is Changli / Brant / Lupa. WaveKit can also suggest owned alternatives through the team helper.

Is Changli beginner friendly?

Changli's current difficulty is listed as Beginner friendly. If you are learning, pair them with safer sustain or defensive support.
