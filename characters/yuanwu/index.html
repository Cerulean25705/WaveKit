Yuanwu is an Electro Defensive Support in Wuthering Waves. Its recommended build starts with Verity's Handle, while its Echo plan uses Empyrean Anthem. The guide also covers stat priorities, compatible teammates, and a practical rotation.

Yuanwu FAQ

What is the best Yuanwu build in Wuthering Waves?

Yuanwu's recommended WaveKit setup is Sub-DPS Build, using Verity's Handle, Empyrean Anthem, and Nightmare: Tempest Mephis as the main Echo target.

What stats should Yuanwu use?

Start with CRIT Rate or CRIT DMG / Electro DMG / DEF%. If the rotation feels uncomfortable, improve Energy Regen or defensive comfort before chasing perfect substats.

What teams work with Yuanwu?

Yuanwu is flexible, so the best team depends on the Resonators and weapons you own.

Is Yuanwu beginner friendly?

Yuanwu's current difficulty is listed as Low stress. If you are learning, pair them with safer sustain or defensive support.
