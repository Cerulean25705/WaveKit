Luuk Herssen FAQ

What is the best Luuk Herssen build in Wuthering Waves?

Luuk Herssen's recommended WaveKit setup is Tune Strain Basic ATK DPS Build, using Daybreaker's Spine, Rite of Gilded Revelation, and Twin Nova - Nebulous Cannon as the main Echo target.

What stats should Luuk Herssen use?

Start with Crit Rate or Crit DMG / Spectro DMG / ATK%. If the rotation feels uncomfortable, improve Energy Regen or defensive comfort before chasing perfect substats.

What teams work with Luuk Herssen?

A strong reference shell is Luuk Herssen / Denia / Mornye. WaveKit can also suggest owned alternatives through the team helper.

Is Luuk Herssen beginner friendly?

Luuk Herssen's current difficulty is listed as Beginner friendly. If you are learning, pair them with safer sustain or defensive support.
