Aalto FAQ

What is the best Aalto build in Wuthering Waves?

Aalto's recommended WaveKit setup is DPS-Support Build, using The Last Dance, Moonlit Clouds, and Impermanence Heron as the main Echo target.

What stats should Aalto use?

Start with CRIT DMG or CRIT Rate / Aero DMG or Energy Regen / ATK%. If the rotation feels uncomfortable, improve Energy Regen or defensive comfort before chasing perfect substats.

What teams work with Aalto?

Aalto is flexible, so the best team depends on the Resonators and weapons you own.

Is Aalto beginner friendly?

Aalto's current difficulty is listed as Flexible. If you are learning, pair them with safer sustain or defensive support.
