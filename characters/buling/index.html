Simple play pattern

  • Build enough Energy Regen so Buling can heal before panic moments.
  • Use support/healing setup first, then leave field time for the damage dealer.
  • Prioritise consistency over perfect damage if you are still learning fights.

Buling FAQ

What is the best Buling build in Wuthering Waves?

Buling's Skill damage support build uses Rejuvenating Glow and Fallacy of No Return, with Stringmaster as the strongest listed personal option.

How much Energy Regen does Buling need?

Aim for roughly 160-180% before S2, or around 125-135% at S2 and above. Stop adding Energy Regen once her rotation is reliable.

What teams work with Buling?

Buling is best used with Resonance Skill damage carries such as Carlotta or Phrolova rather than as a universal healer.

Is Buling a full healer?

Buling provides healing, but her specialist value is buffing Resonance Skill damage. Use a universal sustain when the team does not benefit from that part of her kit.
