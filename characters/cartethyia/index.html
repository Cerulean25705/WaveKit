Team direction

Cartethyia is strongest when the team feeds Aero Erosion. Ciaccona is the premium enabler, while Chisa or Aero Rover handle the Erosion support slot when owned.

Simple play pattern

  • Set up both teammates before giving Cartethyia the main damage window.

  • If the team feels stressful, use the helper to choose a real healer or safer third slot.
