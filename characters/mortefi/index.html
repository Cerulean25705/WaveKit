Mortefi FAQ

What is the best Mortefi build in Wuthering Waves?

Mortefi's recommended WaveKit setup is Coordinated ATK Sub-DPS Build, using Static Mist, Empyrean Anthem, and Hecate as the main Echo target.

What stats should Mortefi use?

Start with CRIT Rate / Fusion DMG or Energy Regen / ATK%. If the rotation feels uncomfortable, improve Energy Regen or defensive comfort before chasing perfect substats.

What teams work with Mortefi?

Mortefi is flexible, so the best team depends on the Resonators and weapons you own.

Is Mortefi beginner friendly?

Mortefi's current difficulty is listed as Flexible. If you are learning, pair them with safer sustain or defensive support.
