Quick answers

Wuthering Waves teams without the pressure.

WaveKit is built for players who want useful team and build direction without needing to read ten pages of meta discussion before they can play.

What is WaveKit?

WaveKit is a fan-made Wuthering Waves team helper that suggests practical teams and builds from the Resonators and weapons you own.

Does it only recommend premium teams?

No. Strong known shells are prioritised when you own them, but WaveKit also shows sensible fallback teams and safer substitutes.

Do I need an account?

No. Profiles can stay local in your browser. Optional cloud sync is only there if you want the same roster across devices.

Data notes

Built to be honest about certainty.

WaveKit is a fan-made helper shaped by released-character data, public theorycrafting, and player reports. Team scoring is conservative: it prefers proven shells and sensible alternatives over risky guesses.

Released roster only Player reports reviewed Patch-sensitive picks marked cautiously No hidden paid recommendations
